About Us

Who We Are

We are a team of enthusiastic students - Alicia, Steeve, Sienna, and Mahjabin - who share a passion for puzzles and cybersecurity. Inspired by our common interests, we created this website to teach others about ciphers and the fascinating world of cryptography.

MASS Encryptor

Purpose: MASS Encryptor is designed to allow users to encrypt messages and decrypt them using common ciphers. It is intended to be a fun and educational tool that engages users in cryptography.

Target Audience & Needs:

General Public & Educational Use

Our target audience ranges from young children who want to play fun games and learn about common ciphers, to adults seeking to exchange private information discreetly, to individuals who enjoy exercising their brains by cracking codes and need a quick checker to verify their solutions.

The ciphers we use are well-known and often employed in fun family games or educational settings to teach kids about ciphers and enhance cybersecurity knowledge.
