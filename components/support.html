FAQ

What is MASS Encryptor?

MASS Encryptor is a tool designed to encrypt and decrypt messages using various ciphers.

How do I use the Translator feature?

To use the Translator feature, navigate to the Translator page and follow the instructions to input your message and select a cipher.

Can I suggest new features?

Yes, we welcome suggestions for new features. Please use the contact form on the Contact page to submit your ideas.

How can I report a bug?

To report a bug, click the 'Report a Bug' button on this page and follow the instructions.
